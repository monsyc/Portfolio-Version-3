Delta Airlines App

Redesign of the app that in the back of the seats

PROBLEM

Delta is an airline with a uniform style. For this project, I had the opportunity to create an original concept for an app, which can potentially be used for the screens on the back part of the seats.

SOLUTION

Illustrator and Photoshop gave me the tools to build this app. All the icons were created from scratch using the same kind of line to have consistency in all the pages.

Being flat design my main concept, I try to represent simplicity and originally in the design, adding other features that makes the app more attractive. One of them is the background; the clouds are constantly moving, and these change depending the daytime and climate.
